Foundations Part 2

Well, the second week of foundations has begun.
After a couple of days off I found time to work on the TestFirst assignments and the solutions came relatively easily to me.

In particular my solution for PigLatin seemed a bit verbose.
Regardless I think that I am already learning a lot from this program.
I had in the past taken another web development course and perhaps because of its destructured nature I found it much harder to retain information.
Anyway signing off, I am mostly putting up this post to make sure I remember how to use hexo.
